Travel by Air

Samos International Airport

Flight Options

  • Most common route: Flight to Athens, then connect to Samos. Alternatively, flight to Thessaloniki, then connect to Samos.
  • During summer season, direct flights are available from some European cities (TODO: Add cities).

We recommend to check [GOOGLE FLIGHTS] as they offer the best coverage of flight connections to Greece (you can also setup alerts for prices and connections).

You can also check directly on the website of the Greek Airlines Aegean (Anastasia's favourite airlines) and Sky Express, as they are most likely to offer flights to Greece and Samos. Additionally, you can also check directly on the website your local airlines (e.g. Lufthansa, Swiss, Lot).

Travel by Sea

Ferry Connections

We recommend https://www.ferryhopper.com/ for booking the ferry. The price is usually between 60 and 90 euro one way. Journey time from Athens is between 10 and 11 hours. The boat journey could be a great experience and opportunity to enjoy Greek landscapes and islands (e.g. Mykonos, Siros, Ikaria).

In Athens the boats to Samos depart from

  • Piraeus

On Samos you can arrive in any of these ports

  • Karlovasi
  • Vathy
